Mademosielle Caroline Riviere

by Jean-August-Dominique Ingres

The potrait of Mademosielle Caroline Riviere was painted in 1806 by the French artist Jean-August-Dominique Ingres and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of the three potraits of the Riviere family the artist painted that year

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimension: 100 * 70 cm
Home: Musee du Louvre, Paris
